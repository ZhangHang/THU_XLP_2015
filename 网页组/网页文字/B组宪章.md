# 创客宪章

## 序言

- 组织名称：创客空间
- 组织定位：开放式的开源代码信息分享管理平台，系统完全由代码运行构建，由一部分成员进行共同维护
- 组织精神：创客空间是本着自由、公平、无私的理念所建立的空间。开源有着高效率，高自由度，足够的创新空间等等一系列优点，我们的核心目的就是通过推广开源代码，推广自由平等的观念。我们也相信开源代码是网络社会的未来。
- 本宪章为保障和促进联盟不断发展，营造自由交流、平等学习、共同分享的国际创新平台，培养愿意动手创造、创新的创客，创建高效率的团队认知学习模式，规范联盟的正常运行机制而设立。我们希望该宪章能够成为该空间的唯一的元语言，指导空间的全部运行。

## Preface

- Organization name： Hacker Space
- Organization Position： Open sources sharing platform. The system is run only by code. While some of the member is in charge of the system maintenance.
- Organization Spirt:Hacker Space is built based on the persuit for freedom, justice, selfless. The open source system have many advantages, including high efficiency, enough freedom, adequate room for innovation。 Our core content is popularizing the concept of freedom and justice by the open source codes. And we believe the open source is the future of the online society. 
- The Charter is set up to safeguard and promote the constant development of the League,and build an international innovate platform on which users can communicate freely,study equally and share jointly.It's set up to train the hackers who has the willing to create or do something innovate,and build a high-efficiency teamstudy mode and standardize the normal orginization system.We hope that the Charter can be the only Metalanguage of our space,then the Charter will guide the space as it runs.

## 第一章	总则
- 创客空间是以尽可能普及与提高开源代码分享的高效率网络平台。
- 创客空间（后简称空间）的主要目的在于提供用户（所有有意愿加入的网民）以分享源代码，公开源代码的空间。并给予正在搜索和整合源代码用以软件开发等的工作的用户方便的工具。
- 本平台也会与其他网络公司相合作，提供只有本平台拥有的开放源代码。

## Chapter1 General Rules
- The Hacker Space is a high efficiency online platform using to popularify the open source system
- The Hacker Space(Space) would provide chances to open and share codes. It will also be a handy tool for the software designer who is endeavor to seek open source codes.
- This platform will also coorperate with other cyber company, which will as result get the open source codes which will only be accessed in the Space.

## 第二章    成员

### 一、加入方式以及身份认证
- 成员通过手机短信或者一定范围的email地址实名注册的方式做出申请，并且会有相关的安全部门对成员的申请进行认证。
- 所有成员均有自由加入团队和退出团队的权利。
- 注册时需签署相关协议；

### 二、成员的权利
- 所有成员可以进入按照自己要求的方式（具体许可方式见附录表格）在网站提供的个人空间中，分享代码，并可使用数字身份完成以上操作。
- 成员可以在个人空间中填写自己的个人信息，并可以通过网站搜索和自己有着相同爱好或是处在相同地域的其他成员。
- 成员能够看到被分享的所有的源代码。但对其具体的操作要遵照所有方的要求。
- 成员可以参加空间组织的线上线下活动，线下活动需依照管理员要求在线上进行提前报名。
- 所有成员可以向空间管理者提出提案，也可以向空间做出自己捐献。
- 拥有一定网络安全知识的成员可以主动申请加入管理人员的安全部门，通过测试后可以进入安全部门，空间也会为其提供一定的酬金。
- 成员浏览完源代码可以在下方对源代码内容进行审核

### 三、成员的义务
- 成员利用本平台提供的开源代码修改的得到的代码或软件等成果，被要求在本平台开源分享，其具体要求仍然可以自行设定。
- 由使用本平台开源代码得出成果所得任何利润，应当将所得利润的5%分享给原来代码提供者。如提供者代码也是由另一成员提供的，提供者将其获得的金额的5%分享给原提供者。如果源代码是空间提供的，将5%交至平台用于系统维护以及购买更多源代码。
- 空间中所有人员未经允许不得私自将其他成员的代码转载，修改，一经发现发现，该成员将被去除所有代码的浏览权限一周，如有情节严重者，直接强制退出空间。

## Chapter 2 Member(Hacker)

### No.1 the method to join and the identity confirmation
- 1.Everyone could submit by using cell phone message or e-mail address to guarantee the real-name registration. And the relevant security department will in charge of the confirmation of the application.
- 2.Every hacker could quit the hacker space follow his or her own will.
- 3.You should sign a protocol when you apply to register.

### No.2 The Right of The Hacker
- 1. Every hacker could upload and share his or her code in the Personal Room the Space provided. He or she could limit others operation acording to the chart in the appendix. During the operation, the electronical ID is needed.
- 2. Every hacker could fulfill the personal information. Also they could search for the hacker who share the same hobby or in the same city.We provide hackers the opportunity to make friends.
- 3. All the hackers could see all the open sources in the Space. However, the operation upon them would be limited by the specific requet of the provider.
- 4. Every hacker could participate the activities the Space organizes, both online and offline. For the offline activities, one should register to the manager on the relevent pages.
- 5. Every hacker could submit proposal or do his or her own contribution by giving open source free.
- 6. The hacker with network security knowledge could apply to attend the security department. After passing our test, we would offer he or her some job and also some payment. 

### No.3 The Obligation of The Hacker
- 1. the product of the hacker must be uploaded and shared to the Space if it relied on the open sources from others on the Space.He or she still could limit others operation
- 2. The 5% profit from any product above must be given to the owner of the previous open source. If the previous open soure is also product above, the owner should share 5% of the profit he got. 
- 3. No one should modify or transmit the code without allowance. If one violate, he will be punished in the corresponding way. 

## 第三章	管理制度

### 第一条. 管理员选举
- 每个月举行线上选举，选举出管理员管理；（后面会单独说明管理员职责）
- 初步设定，管理员人数为2/1000;且管理员人数不低于5人；

### 第二条. 成员可以在动议模版上提交动议，线上投票。
- 普通动议一天后统计结果
- 紧急动议成员自主设定时间，提交管理员审核后发布并开通提醒
- 统计结果超过50%赞成动议通过

### 第三条.	日常维护
- 日常维护所有内容在日常维护中公开；
- 本系统建立完成后，由系统在任务版上按设定发布平台日常维修任务，全体成员可以领取任务并完成。
- 动议模版上分设任务申请动议，当成员发现系统漏洞，可申请为任务，投票和其他动议一致，通过后系统自动将其提交到任务模版，成员可自主认领；
- 管理员在权责范围内管理维护系统；

### 第四条.任何成员如果发现其他成员的不当行为（例如利用黑客技术进行剽窃，或者诽谤），可在维权板块上向管理员申请维权；
- 如果对管理员处理结果有异议，可以提交大众评审，在动议模版上的维权动议模块提交维权动议，投票和一般动议一致；
- 需要至少三个管理员处理
- 如果对大众评审结果有异议，可以向法律部门提出申请；
- 具体惩治见后；

### 第五条.管理员职权（除作为成员外的职权的额外职权）：
- 审核紧急动议
- 对成员提交的诉讼进行处理

### 第六条.公开制度
- 以上管理均在模块上公开

### 第七条.惩制措施
-	措施1：关小黑屋1――取消在平台上的下载相关权限一段时间，但仍然能阅读浏览资料，并可以通过上传一定好评率的资料来提前释放；
-	措施2：关小黑屋2――取消在平台上的阅读下载等一切相关权限一段时间，并可以通过上传一定好评率的资料来提前释放；
-	措施3：关小黑屋3――取消在平台上的下载相关权限一段时间，但仍然能阅读浏览资料，但是不可以通过上传一定好评率的资料来提前释放；
-	措施4：关小黑屋4――取消在平台上的阅读下载等一切相关权限一段时间，但是不可以通过上传一定好评率的资料来提前释放；
-	措施5：冻结身份帐号甚至取消数字身份

## Chapter 3 Regulation Methods

### No 1. Manager Election.
- Online elections held every month, the election decides the manager;
- (This will be described separately manager responsibilities behind.)
- Preliminary setting, the manager for the number 2/1000 percent of  the total number and the administrator number not less than 5 people.

### No.2. Hackers can submit a motion in motion template, voting online.
- For a general motion ,the manager should give statistical results.
- For emergency motion, hacker independent set time, submitted to the manager audit after the release and open to remind.
- Statistical results need that the ratio of  the people who agree with it more than 50% to move through the motion.

### No.3. Daily maintenance
- Daily maintenance of all need to be broadcast to all hackers.
- Content in the day-to-day maintenance;
- After this system has been completed, the system will publish daily maintenance tasks which set by publishing platform.
- All the hackers can receive tasks and complete them.
- Motion template respectively task to apply to move, when the hackers find loopholes in the system, can apply for a task,
- Voting and other motions uniformly, by the system automatically submits it to the task template,hackers may be of independent claim;
- manager within the scope of responsibility management and maintenance;

### No.4. Any hacker if it is found that the improper behavior of other members (such as the use of hacker technology for plagiarism, or slander), may apply to the administrator rights in the Uygur power plate;
- If there is any discrepancy in the result of the manager, you can submit a public review, submitted activist motion module on motion template rights initiative.And the  vote result should be in consistent with general motion. 
- You need at least three manager to deal with the case. 
-If  someone has different option with the audit results, the public may apply to the legal department; 
-Something on punishment will be listed after. 

### No.5 The power of manager
- Verify the emergency motion.
- Deal with the lawsuit of the members.

### No.6 Transparency management
- All the management system and the actual management above can be seen online

### No.7 Punishment
- measure1. The black hut_level 1:Cancel the hacker's ability to download the codes, while he or she could still read them. Could be unfreezed if upload some file which have good comment.
- measure2. The black hut_level 2:Cancel the hacker's ability to download and read the codes. Could be unfreezed if upload some file which have good comment.
- measure3. Prison_Level 1:Cancel the hacker's ability to download the codes, while he or she could still read them. Couldn't be unfreezed if upload some file which have good comment.
- measure4. Prison_Level 2:Cancel all the right of the hacker. Couldn't be unfreezed if upload some file which have good comment.
- measure5. Froze the account or cancel the digital ID

## 第四章	对外制度
- 创客空间内部的资源，对内按所遵循的协议开放，对外默认完全不开放，属于创客空间的内部资产。
- 创客有权在相应协议的基础上处理内部资源，对外盈利或公开；但所进行的处理必须写入控制平台上报，且收益属于创客空间，不得私占。违反者轻则冻结账号，重则追究其法律责任。
- 创客空间成员严禁外泄其他成员资料及内部机密，否则追究相应法律责任；创客只有权力任意处置自己的个人信息，且创客空间及其他成员不承担造成的一切经济和法律责任。
- 创客空间全体成员有义务维护空间相对外部的安全。如遇外部攻击，处理同系统漏洞，即，成员应当将其提交申请为任务，投票通过后将进入任务模板，其他成员可自主认领；如情况紧急，成员应立即申请为紧急动议。

## Chapter 4 Regulations of External Relationship
- Resources inside Hackers' Space, is open to all hackers based on the relevant licenses, and is totally externally closed as a default setting. They belong to Hackers' Space.
- Hackers have the right to deal with internal resources based on the relevant licenses, to make profits or simply publish it. But all the behavior should be written into the control platforms, and all the profits made only belong to Hackers' Space. Occupying without others agreement is not allowed. Violators’ accounts should frozen, or shall be investigated for their legal responsibility. 
- Hacker space members should not leak  other hackers' and internal’s confidential information. Otherwise,shall be investigated for legal responsibility accordingly;Hacker space hackers only have power over disposable personal information, and create a Hacker space and other hackers do not bear all economic and legal responsibility. 
- Hacker space members have the duty to protect the safety of the space relative to the external. In case of external attack, treatment with system vulnerabilities, that is to say, hackers shall submit the application for the task, after the vote,hackers will see the task template, other hackers can claim independently; If the case is  emergence, hackers should apply immediately for an emergency motion.
